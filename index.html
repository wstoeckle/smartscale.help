01 — Services What I do

AI and agentic operations that actually run the business.

Most AI tooling is built for the Fortune 500. I focus exclusively on small and mid-sized businesses, where a few well-placed agents can move the bottom line in a quarter.

// automate

Process & back-office automation

Data entry, document processing, invoicing, scheduling, email triage — the unglamorous work that quietly eats payroll. Replaced with agents that work overnight.

// integrate

Systems & tooling integration

Stitch the tools you already use — CRM, ERP, accounting, ops — into one workflow. No rip-and-replace. No six-month implementation. It works with what you have.

// analyze

Business intelligence

Turn the data you're already generating into clear answers: margin by service line, customer trends, operational bottlenecks you can fix this week.

// scale

Growth infrastructure

The systems foundation to grow without proportionally growing headcount. Handle more volume, serve more customers, hold quality steady as you scale.

02 — Method How I work

Embedded operator. Not a software vendor.

I don't sell licenses or hand off implementations. I sit with your team, learn your operations, and build around how the business actually runs — then stay until it's working.

  1. STEP 01

    Understand the business inside out

    I start in the field — talking to your team, riding along, mapping where time and margin are leaking. No assumptions, no decks, no jargon.

  2. STEP 02

    Find the highest-leverage opportunities

    Not everything needs AI. I identify the two or three changes that move the needle, and we focus there before anything else.

  3. STEP 03

    Build and deploy in weeks

    Practical systems your team can actually use on Monday morning. I stay hands-on through rollout — no throwing it over the wall.

  4. STEP 04

    Measure results, then expand

    Real ROI: hours saved, costs reduced, revenue unlocked. Once it's proven, we widen the footprint from there.
